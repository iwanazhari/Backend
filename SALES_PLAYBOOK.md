## 📊 Sales Pipeline Management

### **CRM Stages:**

```
Stage 1: Lead (New Contact)
├── Source: Twitter/LinkedIn/Email/Referral
├── Action: Initial outreach
└── Goal: Start conversation

Stage 2: Qualified (Responded)
├── Criteria: Responded + has need + has budget
├── Action: Discovery call
└── Goal: Understand pain points

Stage 3: Demo (Interested)
├── Criteria: Booked demo call
├── Action: Product demo + presentation
└── Goal: Show value fit

Stage 4: Proposal (Considering)
├── Criteria: Asked for pricing/details
├── Action: Send proposal + follow up
└── Goal: Address objections

Stage 5: Closed Won (Purchased)
├── Criteria: Completed checkout
├── Action: Onboarding sequence
└── Goal: Activation + success

Stage 6: Closed Lost (Not Now)
├── Criteria: Explicitly said no / ghosted
├── Action: Nurture sequence
└── Goal: Future re-engagement
```
