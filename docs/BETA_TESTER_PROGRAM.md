## 🆘 Risk Mitigation

### **Risk 1: Low Application Rate**

**Mitigation:**
- Extend deadline by 1 week
- Increase social media frequency
- Reach out to influencers for amplification
- Run paid ads ($100-200 budget)

### **Risk 2: High Drop-off Rate**

**Mitigation:**
- Weekly check-in emails
- Office hours calls (record for those who can't attend)
- Progress sharing thread (peer accountability)
- Mid-program prize (best progress wins $100)

### **Risk 3: Low Testimonial Quality**

**Mitigation:**
- Provide testimonial template/script
- Offer 1:1 coaching call before recording
- Edit testimonials professionally
- Accept written testimonials as backup

### **Risk 4: Negative Feedback**

**Mitigation:**
- Embrace honest feedback (good or bad)
- Respond publicly to concerns
- Show improvement based on feedback
- Turn critics into advocates (listen + act)
