## 🧠 7 Pricing Psychology Principles

### **1. Anchoring Effect** ✅

**Implementation:**
```
Total Value: $3,995
Today: $199
You Save: $3,796 (95% off)

Perception: "$199 is a steal for $3,995 value!"
```

**Where Applied:**
- Value stack section
- Pricing page hero
- Email sequences
- Checkout page

---

### **2. Decoy Effect** ✅

**Implementation:**
```
Starter: $149 (decoy - limited features)
Pro: $299 $199 (TARGET - best value)
Agency: $999 (premium anchor)

Result: 60-70% choose Pro tier
```

**Why It Works:**
- Starter makes Pro look like upgrade
- Pro at $199 (launch) vs $299 (regular) = obvious win
- Agency at $999 makes Pro seem reasonable

---

### **3. Charm Pricing** ✅

**Implementation:**
```
Option A: $147 / $297 / $997 (odd numbers)
Option B: $149 / $299 / $999 (premium odd)

Test both to see which converts better.
```

**Psychology:**
- Left-digit effect: $197 feels cheaper than $200
- Odd numbers signal "deal" or "discount"

---

### **4. Urgency & Scarcity** ✅

**Implementation:**
```
Time-based:
⏰ Launch Pricing Ends In: [47d 23h 59m 59s]
Regular: $299 → Today: $199 (Save $100)

Quantity-based:
🎟️ Early Bird: 37/100 spots claimed
Only 12 licenses left at this price
```

**Where Applied:**
- Pricing page banner
- Email sequences
- Checkout page
- Social media posts

---

**Implementation:**

### **6. Risk Reversal** ✅

**Implementation:**
```

If you don't save at least 20 hours, email us for 
a full refund. No questions asked.

You keep the code. No hard feelings.

The risk is 100% on us. The upside is 100% yours."
```

**Placement:**
- Below pricing tiers
- Near CTA buttons
- In email sequences
- On checkout page
