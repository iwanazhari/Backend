## 🆘 Handling Negative Feedback

### **Response Template:**

```markdown
Subject: Re: Your Feedback

Hi {{firstName}},

Thank you so much for your honest feedback. I really appreciate it.

You mentioned [specific concern]. I hear you, and here's what I'm doing about it:

[Action 1]
[Action 2]
[Timeline for fix]

Your feedback is making this product better for everyone.

As a thank you, I'd like to offer:
- Extended beta period (free)
- 1:1 onboarding call
- [Other compensation]

Would you be open to a quick call to discuss further?

Best,
{{YourName}}
```

### **Turning Critics into Advocates:**

```
1. Listen actively (don't defend)
2. Acknowledge their frustration
3. Take action (show you care)
4. Follow up (close the loop)
5. Over-deliver (surprise them)
6. Ask for updated testimonial (if experience improved)
```
