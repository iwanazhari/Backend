## 🚀 Product Hunt Launch Plan

### **4-Week Pre-Launch Timeline:**

```
Week 4 Before: Foundation
├── Create Product Hunt account
├── Build profile (photo, bio, links)
├── Follow 100+ hunters
├── Upvote 20+ products daily
├── Comment on 10+ products
└── Build audience on Twitter/LinkedIn

Week 3 Before: Asset Creation
├── Logo (240x240px)
├── Tagline (max 60 chars)
├── First comment (story)
├── Gallery images (4-6)
├── Demo video (2-3 min)
├── GIFs (3-4)
└── Write all copy

Week 2 Before: Build Hype
├── Teaser campaign begins
├── Reach out to supporters (80 people)
└── Create launch day plan

Week 1 Before: Final Prep
├── Submit to Product Hunt
├── Final teaser push
└── Brief supporters
```

---

### **Launch Day Execution (Hour-by-Hour):**

```
🌅 MORNING (12 AM - 8 AM PST)
12:00 AM: Launch goes live!
  - Upvote immediately
  - Post first comment
  - Share in Discord/Slack

12:05 AM: Notify inner circle
  - Text/WhatsApp to 10 closest supporters

12:30 AM: Email #1 to list
  - "We're live on Product Hunt!"

1:00 AM: Social media blitz
  - Twitter post + thread
  - LinkedIn post
  - Share in relevant Discords

6:00 AM: Check rankings
  - Should be top 3 by now
  - Respond to all comments

8:00 AM: Email #2 (momentum update)
  - "500 upvotes in 8 hours!"

☀️ MID-DAY (8 AM - 4 PM)
9:00 AM: Live demo (Twitter Spaces/LinkedIn Live)

10:00 AM: Press outreach
  - TechCrunch tip line
  - VentureBeat
  - Indie Hackers podcast

12:00 PM: Midday update
  - Share current ranking
  - Thank supporters

2:00 PM: Feature spotlight #2
  - Post about business templates

4:00 PM: Email #3 (urgency)
  - "Almost #1 Product of the Day!"

🌆 EVENING (4 PM - 12 AM)
5:00 PM: Final push begins
  - Countdown posts hourly
  - Personal DMs to supporters

8:00 PM: Email #4 (last call)
  - "4 hours left!"

10:00 PM: Final social blitz
  - "2 hours remaining!"

11:45 PM: Final 15 minutes
  - Live countdown
  - Thank everyone
  - Prepare victory/defeat posts

🎉 POST-LAUNCH (After 12 AM)
12:05 AM: Results announced
  - Victory post (#1) or gratitude post (top 3)

12:30 AM: Thank you email
  - Share results
  - Special offer for supporters

1:00 AM: Celebrate! 🍾
```

---

### **6-Email Launch Sequence:**

```
Email 1: Pre-Launch (2 Days Before)
Subject: "Big announcement in 48 hours 🚀"
Purpose: Build anticipation
CTA: Mark calendar

Email 2: Launch Morning (12:30 AM)
Subject: "🚀 We're live on Product Hunt!"
Purpose: Drive first upvotes
CTA: Upvote + share

Email 3: Momentum Update (8 AM)
Subject: "500 upvotes in 8 hours! 🤯"
Purpose: Show social proof
CTA: Help us reach #1

Email 4: Final Push (4 PM)
Subject: "Almost #1! Need your help 🙏"
Purpose: Create urgency
CTA: Upvote one more time

Email 5: Last Call (8 PM)
Subject: "4 HOURS LEFT! ⏰"
Purpose: Final urgency
CTA: Upvote before midnight

Email 6: Results (Day After)
Subject: "We did it! (Here's what happened) 🎉"
Purpose: Celebrate + extended offer
CTA: Claim extended launch pricing
```
