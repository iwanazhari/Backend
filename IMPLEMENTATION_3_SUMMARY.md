## 🆘 Risk Mitigation

### **Risk 1: Low Application Rate**

**Symptoms:** <200 applications after 7 days

**Solutions:**
- Extend deadline by 1 week
- Increase posting frequency (2x/day)
- Reach out to influencers (10-20 DMs)
- Run paid ads ($100-200 budget)
- Partner with communities (Discord, Slack)

---

### **Risk 2: High Drop-off**

**Symptoms:** <70% active after Week 2

**Solutions:**
- Personal check-in emails
- Peer accountability (buddy system)
- Mid-program prize (best progress wins $100)
- Make office hours mandatory (record for absentees)
- Simplify requirements (reduce friction)

---

### **Risk 3: Low Testimonial Quality**

**Symptoms:** Vague testimonials, no specifics

**Solutions:**
- Provide detailed script/template
- Offer 1:1 coaching call before recording
- Ask specific questions ("How many hours saved?")
- Edit testimonials professionally
- Accept written as backup

---

### **Risk 4: Negative Feedback**

**Symptoms:** Low ratings, critical testimonials

**Solutions:**
- Embrace it (shows authenticity)
- Respond publicly (show you listen)
- Take action (fix issues)
- Follow up (close the loop)
- Turn critics into advocates
